<h1>Compliance & Usage Guidelines</h1>

<hr/>

<h2>1. Purpose of This Document</h2>

<p>
This document defines the <strong>official compliance framework and acceptable usage guidelines</strong>
for the AI Outreach Automation system.
It establishes how the system must be used, governed, and operated to remain
legally compliant, platform-aligned, ethically responsible, and enterprise-safe.
</p>

<p>
This document serves as:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>A usage policy for operators and organizations</li>
  <li>A compliance reference for audits and reviews</li>
  <li>An operational boundary definition for automation behavior</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>2. Compliance Philosophy</h2>

<p>
The AI Outreach Automation system is built on the principle that
<strong>automation must augment human intent without violating trust, law, or platform integrity</strong>.
</p>

<p>
Compliance is enforced through:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Architectural safeguards</li>
  <li>Operational constraints</li>
  <li>Explicit usage limitations</li>
  <li>Human accountability</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>3. Intended and Permitted Use Cases</h2>

<h3>3.1 Permitted Use</h3>

<p>
The system is designed exclusively for the following use cases:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>B2B outbound prospecting</li>
  <li>Professional LinkedIn networking</li>
  <li>Business-to-business email outreach</li>
  <li>Pre-sales conversation initiation</li>
  <li>Early-stage qualification conversations</li>
</ul>

<h3>3.2 Explicitly Excluded Use Cases</h3>

<ul>
  <li>B2C mass marketing</li>
  <li>Political messaging or campaigning</li>
  <li>Financial, medical, or legal advice</li>
  <li>Harassment, manipulation, or coercion</li>
  <li>Impersonation of real individuals</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<hr/>

<h2>5. Data Usage Compliance</h2>

<h3>5.1 Source of Data</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Lead data sourced from third-party providers (e.g., Apollo)</li>
  <li>Research data sourced only from publicly available content</li>
  <li>No private, gated, or credential-protected data used</li>
</ul>

<h3>5.2 Data Minimization</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Only data required for outreach is collected</li>
  <li>No enrichment beyond professional context</li>
  <li>No behavioral profiling or sensitive inference</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>6. AI Usage Governance</h2>

<h3>6.1 AI Role Definition</h3>

<p>
AI is used strictly as:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>A research summarization assistant</li>
  <li>A message drafting assistant</li>
  <li>A conversational response generator</li>
</ul>

<p>
AI is <strong>not</strong> used for:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Decision-making with legal or financial consequences</li>
  <li>Deceptive persuasion</li>
  <li>Psychological manipulation</li>
</ul>

<h3>6.2 AI Output Constraints</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Professional, respectful tone enforced</li>
  <li>No false claims or guarantees</li>
  <li>No impersonation of humans</li>
  <li>No misleading identity representation</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>7. LinkedIn Platform Compliance</h2>

<h3>7.1 Invitation Rules</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Connection requests sent only to relevant professionals</li>
  <li>Volume kept within conservative limits</li>
  <li>Randomized delays to mimic human behavior</li>
</ul>

<h3>7.2 Messaging Rules</h3>

<ul>
  <li>No messages sent without connection acceptance</li>
  <li>One first message per accepted connection</li>
  <li>Replies only triggered by inbound messages</li>
</ul>

<h3>7.3 Prohibited LinkedIn Behaviors</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Scraping private profiles</li>
  <li>Automated endorsements or reactions</li>
  <li>Bulk unsolicited messaging</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>8. Email Outreach Compliance</h2>

<h3>8.1 Acceptable Email Use</h3>

<ul>
  <li>B2B professional communication only</li>
  <li>Clear identification of sender intent</li>
  <li>No deceptive subject lines</li>
</ul>

<h3>8.2 Prohibited Email Use</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Spam campaigns</li>
  <li>High-frequency blasting</li>
  <li>Misrepresentation of identity or affiliation</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>9. Consent and Engagement Boundaries</h2>

<ul>
  <li>LinkedIn messages require explicit connection acceptance</li>
  <li>Replies are always prospect-initiated</li>
  <li>No follow-ups after disengagement signals</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>10. Geographic and Regulatory Considerations</h2>

<h3>10.1 GDPR Alignment</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Lawful basis: legitimate interest (B2B)</li>
  <li>Data minimization enforced</li>
  <li>Right to deletion supported at campaign level</li>
</ul>

<h3>10.2 Jurisdictional Responsibility</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Operator must assess local regulations</li>
  <li>System does not auto-detect jurisdictional laws</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>11. Misuse Prevention & Enforcement</h2>

<h3>11.1 Built-in Safeguards</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Reply limits enforced</li>
  <li>No infinite conversation loops</li>
  <li>Strict workflow sequencing</li>
</ul>

<h3>11.2 Operator Enforcement</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Campaign-level shutdown capability</li>
  <li>Manual intervention at any phase</li>
</ul>

<hr/>

<h2>12. Escalation and Human Intervention</h2>

<ul>
  <li>AI replies cease after defined thresholds</li>
  <li>Complex conversations escalated to humans</li>
  <li>System does not negotiate contracts or pricing</li>
</ul>
